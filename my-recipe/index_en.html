Useful Features

Everything you need for recipe management, provided in a simple and easy-to-use interface.

AI Auto Analysis

Simply enter a recipe URL, and AI automatically extracts the title, ingredients, and cooking steps. Saves you the hassle of manual input.

Save with Images

Upload multiple images with your recipes. Save photos for each cooking step for easy-to-understand management.

Flexible Sorting

Sort freely by alphabetical order, category, or registration date. Quickly access your desired recipe with the index bar.

Category Management

Organize recipes by category such as Japanese, Western, or Chinese cuisine. Visually manage with customizable color coding.

Safari Integration

Save directly to My Recipe from Safari's share button. Easily add recipes you find while browsing.

Native Experience

iOS/Android native app developed with Flutter. Delivers smooth animations and comfortable operability.
