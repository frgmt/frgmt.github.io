Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to common questions about using My Recipe.

What is My Recipe?
My Recipe is a recipe management app powered by AI technology. Simply enter a URL, and the AI will automatically extract recipe information and save it in a beautifully organized format.
How do I add a recipe?
You can add recipes by following these steps:
  • Tap the "+" button at the bottom right of the home screen
  • Enter the URL of the recipe page
  • AI will automatically extract the title, ingredients, and cooking steps
  • Add images if needed
  • Select a category and save
Can I save recipes directly from Safari?
Yes, you can (iOS only). Open the recipe page in Safari, tap the share button, and select "Save to My Recipe" from the action area at the bottom. The app will automatically launch with the URL pre-filled in the recipe addition screen.
How do I edit or delete a recipe?
Swipe left on a recipe from the home screen to reveal the edit and delete buttons. You can also edit from the edit button in the top right corner of the recipe detail screen.
What should I do if a recipe isn't parsed correctly?
Some websites may not be parsed correctly. In that case, you can manually edit the parsed results, or please contact us with the URL through the contact form and we will work to improve it.
Is there a data backup?
Yes, your recipe data is securely stored in the Supabase cloud. If you log in with the same account, you can access your data from a different device.
What's the difference between the free and paid plans?
The free plan allows up to 5 AI recipe analyses per month. With the paid plan (Pro monthly plan), AI analysis is unlimited.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage your subscription from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) settings.
  • iOS: Settings app → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → My Recipe Pro
  • Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → My Recipe Pro
